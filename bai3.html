One particular book which is recommended reading is The Street Lawyer by John Grisham This book is about a lawyer who begins re-evaluating his prioirites in life when a bad incident occures within his law firm. Consequently, he becomes acquainted with the inner city streets, and realizes the harsh existence of the homeless, and vows to give them a chance in the courts. The Stree Lawyer is a great book. It is well written and interesting. Other books by John Grisham include The Firm, The Pelican Brief, and The Client.
